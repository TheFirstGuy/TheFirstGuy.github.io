Welcome

Hello and welcome to my web portfolio. Here you will find information about me, my life story thus far, and professional accomplishments. You are welcome to contact me with any questions or comments. Please report any bugs or errors that you find on this website (it is still very much under construction).

Origin

I was born in Switzerland. I was raised there for two years before my family moved to New Jersey when I was three. As a child I spent a large portion of my time dreaming about space and saving the universe (I had learnt about the heat death theory too early). Like many other soon-to-be engineers, I played extensively with Legos, trains, and blocks. I would draw schematics of space ships and beg to play video games on the family computer.

Education

Most of my early education took place in the private school Barnstable Academy. I was placed directly in first grade, skipping kindergarten, and was doing long division by second grade. However, during my fifth year of grade school my family moved to a small rural town Kea`au, on the Big Island of Hawaii. This is where I would spend the remainder of middle and high school education. Although academically the Kea`au public school struggled, it did have its advantages. The schools encouraged all the students to be inclusive and appreciative of the variety of cultures present. I was able to partake in unique extra-curricular activities such as Taiko, tropical agriculture, and world class astronomy.

For my undergraduate I am proud to be attending New York University's Tandon School of Engineering, previously NYU Polytechnic School of Engineering, or Poly for short. I currently am in my final semester to completing my Bachelors in Computer Engineering with honors. While I have studying, I have conducted research, participated in Hackathons, and tutored my fellow students. Moving forward, I would like to continue my education, achieving either a doctorate or a masters in Computer Science.

Plans & Goals

In many ways I am an idealist. Therefore, it should not come as a surprise that many of my goals are quite grand. In general I would like to accomplish designing a conscious artificial intelligence. Hopefully one that assists in solving humanities greatest problems and does not destroy the human race or murder people in the process.

More realistically, I would like to contribute to projects either in industry or academics that help progress society in some fashion. If I am able to accomplish this while still doing all the cliche stuff (traveling the world, raising a family, etc), then I will be a happy man.
